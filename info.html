About the Magical Quiz

Welcome to the Magical Quiz! This game is designed to challenge your knowledge of the wizarding world. With every correct answer, you will charge your wand's power bar, bringing you closer to a special message.

How to Play

To begin your quest, simply select a quiz category from the homepage. Each quiz consists of a series of questions with a 60-second timer. Choose your answer carefullyâ€”one wrong move, and the magic might fizzle!

  • Correct Answer: A celebratory confetti burst.
  • Incorrect Answer: A subtle "negative burst" animation appears.

Enabling Audio

To fully immerse yourself in the magic, this quiz includes background music and sound effects. However, for security reasons, modern web browsers do not allow audio to play automatically.

To enable all sounds, simply click on any button on the page. Your first click will grant the browser permission to play audio, and the background music will begin.

  • A Mute/Unmute button is available on the quiz page to control the background music at any time.
